## Entangled Polling Queries

These two GraphQL queries specify an independant `polling parameter`,
But since they share the same cache, if *either* of them is polling,
They both get updated.

  <RegisterApolloProvider httpurl={defaultNatsHttpurl}>
    <MessagesQyPoll pollInterval={2000} maxRows={4}/>
    <MessagesQyPoll pollInterval={3000} maxRows={4}/>
  </RegisterApolloProvider>
